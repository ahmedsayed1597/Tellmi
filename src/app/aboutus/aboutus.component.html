About Site

Tell Me site helps you get more information by obtaining useful services from your employees and friends in complete confidentiality without the sender knowing

FAQs:-

Will Tell Me site reveal the identities of the senders?

Tell Me site will not reveal the identity of the registered senders to users without their consent

Is it a hacker site?!

Frankly speaking, the site does not steal data, but programs and websites that imitate it may do so

Can visitors frankly view my messages?

They can't unless you share your messages yourself

Can messages be answered?

It is not possible to respond at the moment, the status of this option is currently being studied
